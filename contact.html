Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with us

How long does a typical project take?

+

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. Basic renders typically take 3-5 business days, while comprehensive projects with multiple views and animations can take 2-4 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What information do you need to start a project?

+

We typically need architectural drawings (floor plans, elevations), material specifications, design references, and any specific requirements for camera angles or lighting. The more detailed information you provide, the better we can understand your vision.

Can you work with international clients?

+

Absolutely! We work with clients worldwide. Our communication tools and project management systems make it easy to collaborate regardless of location. We're experienced in working across different time zones.

Do you offer rush services?

+

Yes, we offer expedited services for urgent projects. Rush delivery typically incurs an additional fee of 25-50% depending on the timeline. Contact us with your specific deadline requirements.

What file formats do you deliver?

+

We deliver images in high-resolution JPG, PNG, and PDF formats. For animations, we provide MP4 files. Source files in 3ds Max, Blender, or other formats are available with our Premium package or as an add-on.
