Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our pricing

What's included in the basic package?

+

The basic package includes one exterior view rendered at 4K resolution with basic materials, one revision round, and delivery within 3-5 business days. It's perfect for simple projects or when you need a single high-quality render.

Can I customize my package?

+

Yes! We offer flexible customization options. You can add additional views, upgrade resolution, include source files, or combine multiple services. Contact us for a custom quote tailored to your specific needs.

Do you offer discounts for multiple projects?

+

Absolutely! We offer volume discounts for multiple projects and long-term partnerships. Discounts typically start at 10% for 3+ projects and can go up to 25% for ongoing work.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and PayPal. For larger projects, we can arrange payment plans with 50% upfront and 50% upon completion.

Is there a refund policy?

+

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not satisfied with the initial draft, we'll work with you to make it right. If we can't deliver what you need after multiple revisions, we'll provide a full refund.
