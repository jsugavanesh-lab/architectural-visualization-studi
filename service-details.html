About This Service

Our exterior visualization service transforms your architectural designs into stunning photorealistic renders that capture the essence of your vision. We use advanced 3D modeling and rendering techniques to create images that are indistinguishable from photographs.

Whether you're presenting to investors, marketing to potential buyers, or seeking planning approval, our exterior visualizations help you communicate your design intent with clarity and impact.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

What file formats do you deliver?

+

We deliver images in high-resolution JPG, PNG, and PDF formats. We can also provide source files in 3ds Max, Blender, or other formats upon request.

How many revisions are included?

+

The number of revisions depends on your chosen package. Basic includes 1 revision, Professional includes 3 revisions, and Premium includes unlimited revisions.

What information do you need to start?

+

We typically need architectural drawings (floor plans, elevations), material specifications, design references, and any specific requirements for camera angles or lighting.

Can you work with tight deadlines?

+

Yes, we offer expedited services for urgent projects. Additional fees may apply for rush deliveries. Contact us to discuss your timeline.

Do you provide source files?

+

Source files are available with our Premium package or as an add-on to other packages. This allows you to make future modifications or use the models in other projects.
